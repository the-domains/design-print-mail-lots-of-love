Paper? Bad for he environment?

The Myth: Paper Is Bad For The Environment

The Fact: Paper is one of the few truly sustainable products. Paper is based on wood, a natural and renewable material, and as young trees grow they absorb CO2 from the atmosphere. Wood-based products continue to store carbon throughout their lifetime.
